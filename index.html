2020 Stavnes Open

Saturday, August 29th

Golfers of all skill levels will enjoy their 18-hole golf experience while their abilities are tested in a scenic, natural environment. The golf layout, by acclaimed golf course architects Gill/Miller, is scenic, challenging and incredibly well maintained featuring protected wetlands, plenty of water, expansive shapely bunkers and mature forest.
